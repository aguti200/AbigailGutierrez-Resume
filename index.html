Youth Services Librarian

Hello, I am Abigail Gutierrez. I recently graduated from university with a bachelor's degree. As an undergrad I was invovled in various art and social organizations which allowed me to meet all kinds of people and move out of my comfort zone.

These experiences were fun and engaging, all aspects that I believe work well in a library space. My time volunteering at the library was always a pleasant time because I would meet children of all ages who enjoy reading books. I truly enjoy the atmosphere in a library space as well as encouraging young readers to read. Also, it is nice to know that the library is a center that provides an abundance of resources for the community.

Libraries are the center of knowledge, navigated by librarians who strive to serve its community; it is meaningful work, and I love it!
